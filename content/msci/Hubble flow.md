---
title: "Hubble flow"
tags: ["cosmology"]
--- 

The Hubble flow contributes to observed [[redshift|redshifts]].

However, the [[peculiar velocity]] of an object also contributes.

The net result is

$$
1 + z_{obs} = (1 + z_{\cos})\left( 1 - v_{\mid\mid} \frac{\mathbf{r}}{c} \right)^{-1}
$$
